# Introduction to VLA Systems

## What is VLA?

Vision-Language-Action (VLA) represents the convergence of three AI capabilities that enable robots to perceive, understand, and act on natural language commands.

### The Three Pillars

1. **Vision**: Computer vision for environment perception
2. **Language**: Natural language understanding and generation
3. **Action**: Physical task execution through robotics

## Real-World Applications

- Home assistants that understand "clean the kitchen"
- Warehouse robots responding to "move boxes to aisle 3"
- Healthcare robots helping with "fetch the medication"
